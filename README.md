# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI** dashboard developed to analyze e-commerce sales performance and provide actionable business insights through data visualization.

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset to evaluate business performance across products, customers, sales, and profitability. The dashboard enables users to explore key metrics through interactive visualizations, helping identify sales trends, top-performing products, and opportunities for business growth.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze profit and revenue trends
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
- Compare sales across regions and time periods
- Support data-driven business decisions
